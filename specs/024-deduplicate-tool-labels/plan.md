## Summary

Consolidate three separate tool-name-to-label mappings into a single source of truth (`TOOL_DISPLAY_NAMES` in `src/ai/tools.ts`). Update `toolDisplayLabel()` in `src/ai/workflow-state.ts` to import and read from `TOOL_DISPLAY_NAMES` (replacing its private `TOOL_LABELS` record), and update `toolLabel()` in `src/lessons/generator.ts` to use `TOOL_DISPLAY_NAMES` as its base mapping, keeping only input-aware overrides in its switch statement. Merge missing entries from the other two into `TOOL_DISPLAY_NAMES`. Delete the `TOOL_LABELS` record from `workflow-state.ts`. No behavioral changes -- labels remain identical.
